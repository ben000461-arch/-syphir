Features

Everything you need to
protect your team

Built for small businesses that handle sensitive data daily — without the complexity or price tag of enterprise solutions.

40+ PII entity types

SSNs, credit cards, medical records, API keys, DOBs, EINs, IBAN numbers, driver's licenses, and more — all detected with precision.

Real-time interception

Scans happen before the prompt is submitted. No data leaves the browser until the employee confirms the risk is intentional.

Per-org incident dashboard

Each business sees only their own incidents. Complete isolation — your data never mixes with another organization's.

Zero sensitive data stored

Syphir only logs metadata — entity type, risk score, timestamp. The actual sensitive text never touches our servers.

Works on all major AI tools

ChatGPT, Claude, Grok, Gemini, Copilot, Perplexity — and any new tool can be added to the allowlist with one config change.

One-link employee onboarding

Share one pre-linked URL with your team. They install in 60 seconds with no account, no key to type, no configuration.

Custom detection policies

Turn on or off specific PII types, set custom keywords, configure alert thresholds, and choose how employees are notified.

Live incident feed

See detections in real time as they happen across your team. Filter by employee, AI tool, risk level, or date range.

CSV export & audit logs

Export your full incident history for compliance audits, HIPAA reporting, or internal reviews with one click.
