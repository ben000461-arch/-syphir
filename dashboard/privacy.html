Privacy Policy
Last updated: May 2025

1. Information We Collect

Syphir collects the minimum information necessary to provide the Service. This includes:

  • Organization information: organization name and admin email address provided at signup
  • Employee information: employee email addresses used to identify who triggered a detection event
  • Detection events: when the extension detects sensitive data in an AI prompt, we log the type of data detected (e.g., "SSN"), a masked version of the value, the AI tool name, a timestamp, and a risk level — never the full prompt text
  • Usage metadata: browser information and page URL at the time of detection

We do not collect, transmit, or store the full text of any AI prompts. All PII scanning is performed locally within the browser extension.

2. How We Use Information

Information collected is used solely to provide the Syphir Service:

  • To generate incident reports and populate the admin dashboard
  • To send email alerts to administrators when high-risk incidents occur
  • To maintain account records and process billing
  • To improve detection accuracy and the Service overall

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to operating the Service.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored securely in Supabase, a cloud database provider operating on US-based servers. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Access to the database is restricted to authenticated requests using row-level security policies that scope each organization's data exclusively to that organization.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for commercial purposes. We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Supabase (database), Resend (transactional email), and Stripe (payment processing) receive the minimum data necessary to operate their respective services
  • Legal requirements: if required by law, court order, or government authority
  • Business transfers: in the event of a merger or acquisition, data may be transferred to the successor entity subject to the same privacy commitments

5. Data Retention

Incident logs are retained according to your subscription plan:

  • Starter plan: 30 days of incident history
  • Professional plan: 1 year of incident history
  • Institution plan: unlimited incident history

Upon account cancellation, data is retained for 30 days to allow for export, then permanently deleted. You may request immediate deletion at any time by contacting us.

6. Employee Privacy

Syphir is a monitoring tool deployed by employers. Organizations using Syphir should inform their employees that their AI tool usage is being monitored, in accordance with applicable employment and privacy laws in their jurisdiction. Syphir does not control how organizations deploy or disclose the use of the Service to their employees.

7. Cookies and Local Storage

Syphir uses browser localStorage and chrome.storage.local for session management only — to store your license key and maintain your login session. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies. No data stored locally is shared with or accessible to third parties.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the data we hold about your organization
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at syphir26@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify customers of material changes by email. Continued use of the Service after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

10. Contact

Questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy should be directed to syphir26@gmail.com.
