What it unlocks

Network-level protection

Two dashboard sections come alive the moment your Node is online — the same ones you're previewing now.

⬡

Network Security

Live threat detection across every device on your network. Spot port scans, brute-force attempts, data exfiltration, and connections to known malicious servers — then isolate the device with one click, without cutting its Shield connection.

⌘

Intel commands

Run security actions in plain English straight from your dashboard. "Isolate John's computer." "Block that IP." "Show me what talked to the internet last hour." The Node carries out the command and reports back.

◉

Sees what the browser can't

The extension only sees browser activity. The Node watches every device — laptops, phones, printers, anything on the network — so shadow devices and background traffic stop being blind spots.

🔒

Runs on-site, privately

Monitoring happens on the device in your office. No mirroring your whole network to the cloud — the Node processes traffic locally and sends only the alerts that matter to your dashboard.

Setup

Plug it in. That's it.

No IT project, no rewiring. Built for small teams without a security team.

01

Plug in the Node

Connect it to power and your router. It provisions itself and links to your Syphir account automatically.

02

Enter your routing number

Type the code from the sticker on the bottom of the box into your dashboard. Network Security and Intel switch on.

03

Watch it work

Threats show up live, scored by confidence before any action is taken. Isolate, block, or investigate from the dashboard.
