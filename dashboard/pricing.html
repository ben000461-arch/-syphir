FAQ
Common questions
Is there a free trial?
Yes — all plans come with a free 30-day pilot. No credit card required. We'll reach out to set it up after you submit your email.
Does Syphir store the text my employees type?
No. Syphir only logs metadata — entity type (e.g. "SSN detected"), risk level, timestamp, and AI tool used. The actual prompt text never leaves the browser or touches our servers.
How does the per-org data isolation work?
Every incident is tagged with your organization's ID (derived from your license key). All dashboard queries filter exclusively by that ID. Business A cannot see Business B's data even though they're on the same platform.
Can I add employees after signing up?
Yes, unlimited. Just share your install link and new employees are automatically linked to your org. Your plan determines the max number of active employees.
Does it work on browsers other than Chrome?
The extension currently supports Chrome, Edge, and Brave. Firefox support is on the roadmap.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Your key stays active until the end of your billing period.
