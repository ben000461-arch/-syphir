How it works

Simple by design.
Powerful by default.

No IT department, no complex setup, no training required. Syphir is built so that any small business can deploy enterprise-grade AI data protection in minutes.

01

Your business gets a unique license key

When you sign up, Syphir generates a unique key for your organization — something like SYP-DENT-2026-A3B7. This key is your org's identity in the system. It gates your dashboard, links your employees, and scopes all incident data exclusively to you.

Your key never expires unless you cancel. It's stored in your dashboard under Settings → License Key and in your welcome email.

02

Share one install link with your employees

Your dashboard generates a pre-linked install URL that has your key already embedded in it. You copy it and send it to your staff however you like — email, Slack, a group text, whatever you use.

https://syphir.vercel.app/install.html?key=SYP-YOUR-ORG-KEY

Employees click the link, download the Chrome extension, and install in about 60 seconds. They never need to type a key or create an account.

03

The extension watches every AI prompt in real time

Once installed, Syphir Shield runs silently in the background on ChatGPT, Claude, Grok, Gemini, Copilot, Perplexity, and more. Before any prompt is submitted, it scans the text for 40+ types of sensitive data including SSNs, credit card numbers, patient records, API keys, dates of birth, and more.

If something is flagged, a banner appears instantly — before the data leaves the browser — giving the employee a chance to remove it. They can also acknowledge and proceed, which gets logged.

04

Every incident is logged to your dashboard only

All detections from all your employees flow into your organization's dashboard in real time. You see the AI tool used, the risk level, timestamp, and what type of data was found — but never the actual sensitive text itself (only masked snippets).

Your data is completely isolated. Business A cannot see Business B's incidents, even though they're on the same platform. Each org's key scopes every query exclusively to that org.
